Frequently Asked Questions




What is GitHub? Why use it? ▼

GitHub is a platform that allows you to host and manage your website for free. It's also great for keeping track of your changes, and making sure your website is safe and secure with version control. I use GitHub to help you build your site, and you'll have full control over it once it's set up.

Do I need to know how to code? ▼

No! I will guide you step by step. If you're just getting started, we will work together to ensure you understand how things work, even if you're a complete beginner. If you're more experienced, I can help you with more advanced features and customizations, but it's not required for me to help you.

How does the domain and hosting process work? ▼

When you purchase a service and after we've set up your site, you'll have the option to purchase a domain name or hosting plan. You can use a cheap domain registrar like Namecheap or GoDaddy to purchase a domain name, and you can use GitHub to host your site for free. Or, you can use a paid hosting service like Bluehost or SiteGround. Either way, I'll help you set up your domain and hosting, and make sure it's live on the web.

Can I update my website after it's built? ▼

Absolutely! With GitHub, you'll have complete control over your website and be able to update it whenever you like. I'll show you how to make changes, add new pages, and keep your site up to date. Once you feel comfortable and do not need my help, you can continue to manage your site on your own at anytime, and you're always welcome to contact me if you have further questions.

What is Tech Support Service? ▼

Technical support includes help with setting up your devices, troubleshooting issues, and general advice on how to keep your devices running smoothly. I can help you with your computer, phone, tablet, or other devices (up to two devices per service purchase). This is great for those who aren't building a site, but need help with their devices.

How does payment work? ▼

When you click the "Purchase" button, you'll be redirected to PayPal to complete your payment. You can pay with a credit/debit card, Venmo or PayPal account. Once your payment is processed, you'll be redirected to a Thank You page that explains the next steps. You'll also receive an email with a link to schedule your first session with me. If you have any questions or issues with payment, feel free to reach out contact me using the button below.

Can I get a refund if I don't like it? ▼

The Terms of Service specifies that there are no refunds unless the service was not delivered as described. However, I am here to help you should you have any concerns with any services rendered and we will work towards a solution.

How long does the service cover me for? ▼

Up to 2 years! The service covers you for one website setup. If you are setting up more than one website, you can purchase additional services if needed. I'm here to help you with any questions or issues you may have, and I want to make sure you're comfortable managing your site on your own. Even after the initial setup, you can reach out to me for help at any time at no extra cost. And, you will have permanent access to your site and hosting after the two-year period.

What if I have a question that's not listed here? ▼

If you have any questions that aren't answered here, feel free to reach out to me through this contact form. I'm happy to help with any questions or concerns you may have. For detailed documentation of the terms related to the Service, please refer to the Terms of Service.
