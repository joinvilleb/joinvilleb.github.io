Frequently Asked Questions




Do I need any technical skills to work with you? ▼

No technical skills required! My offering is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to everyone. I handle the entire setup for you, so you can focus on what you do best.

How long does it take for my site to go live? ▼

The process generally takes between 1-2 weeks, depending on the complexity of the site. I'll keep you updated on the progress and make sure you're satisfied with the final product.

Can I request updates or changes to my website after it goes live? ▼

Yes, I provide 18 months of unlimited support for updates, changes, or troubleshooting. I'm here to help you succeed online, even after the site is live.

Will the website be optimized for mobile and SEO? ▼

Absolutely! Every website I create is optimized for mobile devices and search engines. This ensures your site is accessible to everyone and has the potential to rank higher in search results, which is great for your online presence.

How secure will my website be once it's live? ▼

Security is a priority. Every setup I complete includes basic security practices, and I can offer advice on keeping your website secure over time.

Do you offer support after the website is live? ▼

Yes, I provide 18 months of ongoing support to answer any questions and resolve any issues that may arise after launch. I'm here to ensure your site continues to work perfectly, as I understand that things happen.

Will you help me purchase a domain, and is it included in the setup? ▼

Yes, the Full Setup includes custom domain setup, covering up to 50% of the original package price. If you already have a domain, I can help connect it to your new website.

Are there any hidden costs after purchasing the package? ▼

No hidden costs! The Full Setup covers all essential setup fees, including domain costs up to 50% of the package price.
