Hello, I'm Billy Joinville!

Hometown: Camden, Delaware

Currently: Miami, Florida


Born and raised in Delaware, I'm a current student at the University of Miami pursuing degrees in Computer Science and Communication Studies. To understand the future of our planet and how organizations are contributing, I added a minor in Sustainable Business.

My passion lies in creating and contributing to the future of technology and how it can be used to solve problems and improve lives. My future goals include working in the tech industry for an esteemed and driven company, where I can continue to learn and grow as a professional.

I'm also a member of Alpha Kappa Psi, the professional business fraternity, where I've had the opportunity to network with professionals and develop my leadership skills.

❮ ❯

As I move forward in my academic and professional journey, I've had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects and take on leadership roles that bring together my technical and organizational skills. That includes being an active listener & collaborating on new ideas, understanding languages like Python, Java, and JavaScript, and building connections along the way.

During my time at the University of Miami, I've had the opportunity to intern at companies like Delaware Elevator and participate in events like eMerge Americas. I'm dedicated to making an impact on everything I do, and I invite you to learn more about my work.
