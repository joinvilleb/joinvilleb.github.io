In order to set up an appointment with me, you would have to come in-person, to my residence. My residence is located at 1280 Stanford Drive, Coral Gables, FL 33146. My hours of operation are from 8am - 3pm on Mondays through Fridays. All appointments will be held during that time-frame. You can request an appointment time outside of 8am - 3pm, but that will incur an additional fee of $50.

Canceling and rescheduling appointments
If you need to cancel your appointment, you must cancel within 24 hours before your appointment time, or else you will be charged a non-refundable fee of $150.
Rescheduling appointments must be done in person. I am not allowed to share my phone number on this website due to the risk of spam calls that may occur from scam artists.
If there is a situation where you are not in control of canceling the appointment, please notify us as soon as possible - and we can make necessary arrangements.
Refund policy
There are no refunds. All sales are final. If you have any questions or concerns, please reach out to me at billy@miami.edu.
Keep in mind that when you schedule an appointment, a hold will be authorized on your account - and will be charged after your appointment. This is to avoid risk of any appointment cancelations that are not done within the 24 hour period.
