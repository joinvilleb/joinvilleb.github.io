How do sessions work?

Sessions are a support method to work "face-to-face" on your website. They're held on Zoom and last 30 minutes. You can schedule up to 5 sessions per month.

Cancellation policy: Life happens and we're human. If something comes up, cancel or reschedule your session at least 12 hours in advance.
