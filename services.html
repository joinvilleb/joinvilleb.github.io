Frequently Asked Questions




What is GitHub? Why use it? ▼

GitHub is a platform that allows you to host and manage your website for free. It's also great for keeping track of your changes, and making sure your website is safe and secure with version control. I use GitHub to help you build your site, and you'll have full control over it once it's set up.

Do I need to know how to code? ▼

No! I will guide you step by step. If you're just getting started, we will work together to ensure you understand how things work, even if you're a complete beginner. If you're more experienced, I can help you with more advanced features and customizations, but it's not required for me to help you.

Can I update my website after it's built? ▼

Absolutely! With GitHub, you'll have complete control over your website and be able to update it whenever you like. I'll show you how to make changes, add new pages, and keep your site up to date. Once you feel comfortable and do not need my help, you can continue to manage your site on your own at anytime, and you're always welcome to contact me if you have further questions.
