Frequently Asked Questions



How does the deposit work? ▼

The deposit is 30% of the starting price for the web setup package and is applied toward the final price we agree upon. This deposit secures your project spot and allows work to begin. The remaining balance will be invoiced once the final price is determined (within 30 minutes of the deposit) and will be due within two business days.

Why is your price so low compared to other web designers? ▼

Billy Joinville Web Services (BJWS) believes in providing high-quality services at an affordable price. While Billy Joinville is an active student at the University of Miami, he's able to offer lower prices than other web designers. Every website he creates is an opportunity to learn and grow, and he truly enjoys helping others succeed online. You can learn more about Billy Joinville and BJWS here.

Do I need any technical skills to work with you? ▼

No technical skills required! My offering is designed to be user-friendly and accessible to everyone. I handle the entire setup for you, so you can focus on what you do best.

Do you work with existing websites? ▼

Yes, I can work with existing websites to make updates, changes, or improvements. I'll review your site and provide recommendations for how we can work together to make it even better. The same pricing applies for existing websites as it does for new websites.

How long does it take for my site to go live? ▼

The process generally takes between 1-2 weeks, depending on the complexity of the site. I'll keep you updated on the progress and make sure you're satisfied with the final product.

Can I request updates or make changes to my website after it goes live? ▼

Yes, I provide 18 months of unlimited support for updates, changes, or troubleshooting. You can also customize your site on your own if you feel comfortable doing so. I'm here to help you succeed online, even after the site is live.

What is the refund policy? ▼

No refunds are available once I begin work on your website (this will be confirmed via our first session or via email). If you're not satisfied with the final product, I'll work with you to make sure you're happy with the result. You can view the full refund policy here.
