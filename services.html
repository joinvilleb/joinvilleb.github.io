Frequently Asked Questions




What is GitHub, and why do I need it? ▼

GitHub is a platform that allows you to host and manage your website for free. It's also great for keeping track of your changes, and making sure your website is safe and secure with version control.


Do I need to know how to code? ▼

No! I will guide you step by step. If you're just getting started, we will work together to ensure you understand how things work, even if you're a complete beginner.


Can I update my website after it's built?▼

Absolutely! With GitHub, you'll have complete control over your website and be able to update it whenever you like.
