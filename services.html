Frequently Asked Questions



What is GitHub? Why use it? ▼

GitHub is a platform that allows you to host and manage your website for free. It's also great for keeping track of your changes, and making sure your website is safe and secure with version control. I use GitHub to help you build your site, and you'll have full control over it once it's set up.

Do I need to know how to code? ▼

No! I will guide you step by step. If you're just getting started, we will work together to ensure you understand how things work, even if you're a complete beginner. If you're more experienced, I can help you with more advanced features and customizations, but it's not required for me to help you.

How much time will it take to set up my site? ▼

For Full Setup, it usually takes a week to set up your site, depending on the complexity of your site. Turnarond time is an area where I excel, and I'll make sure your site is up and running as soon as possible.

How does the domain and hosting process work? ▼

When you purchase a service and after we've set up your site, you'll have the option to purchase a domain name or hosting plan. We cover up to $30 USD in domain registration fees, per domain. You can use a cheap domain registrar like Namecheap or GoDaddy to purchase a domain name, and you can use GitHub to host your site for free. Or, you can use a paid hosting service like Bluehost or SiteGround. Either way, I'll help you set up your domain and hosting, and make sure it's live on the web.
