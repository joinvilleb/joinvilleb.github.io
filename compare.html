Portfolio

A portfolio site is a website that showcases your work, whether that be photography, writing, or other creative endeavors. It's a way to show off your skills and experience to potential clients or employers.

I can help you create a portfolio that highlights your skills through personalized stories, custom visuals, etc. All tailored to reflect your authentic self.




Small Business

A small business site is a website that promotes your business, products, and services. It's a way to attract new customers and build your brand online.

If you're looking to start or maintain a small business, I can help you create a site that reflects your brand, and helps you connect with your customers online.
